------------------------------------------------------------
THE DO'S ✅
------------------------------------------------------------
[1] DO audit your privacy settings regularly to control 
    who can see your personal posts and tagged photos.
    
[2] DO use a professional profile picture and bio on 
    platforms like LinkedIn to attract recruiters.
    
[3] DO think before you post; ask yourself if you would 
    be comfortable with a recruiter reading it.
    
[4] DO network with industry professionals and alumni 
    to build a digital community within your field.
    
[5] DO fact-check information before sharing to maintain 
    credibility and prevent the spread of misinformation.

------------------------------------------------------------
THE DON'TS ❌
------------------------------------------------------------
[1] DON'T post illegal or policy-violating content, such 
    as underage drinking or illicit substances.
    
[2] DON'T engage in "flame wars" or online arguments that 
    can be screenshotted and used against you later.
    
[3] DON'T share sensitive personal info, like your home 
    address, student ID, or specific class locations.
    
[4] DON'T complain about your school or job publicly; 
    keep professional grievances private or internal.
    
[5] DON'T use offensive language or post discriminatory 
    content that could be flagged as harassment.
